Website Evaluations

Good Website: Airbnb

Airbnb has revolutionized the way people travel by providing access to a vast array of unique accommodations and fostering connections between travelers and local communities around the world.

  • Contrast: Beautiful difference in text color and icons to show hierarchy of different headers and elements.
  • Repitition: Same use of color, fonts, and icons throughout website making it consistent.
  • Alignment: Everything is aligned perfectly to make it look aesthetically pleasing, displayed with pictures in grid layout.
  • Proximity: Related photos are placed together in unity there is no unnecessary whitespace making a clean layout.

Bad Website: CNN

Cable News Network, one of the world's largest news organizations. It serves as a comprehensive digital news hub, providing up-to-date coverage of global events, politics, business, entertainment, and more.

  • Contrast: There is minimal contrast and it makes the website look boring with a simple white background and black text.
  • Repitition: There is little consistency with different medias being used and different layouts.
  • Alignment: Lacking in alignment, could be a lot better if it wasn't random.
  • Proximity: A lot of empty whitespace not being used strategically and elements together do no fit well.
